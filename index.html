What is atomic swap?

Atomic swap is a technology that enables the exchange of one cryptocurrency for another without the need for intermediaries or centralized exchanges. Atomic swaps are decentralized and provide a secure and fast way to exchange digital assets while maintaining privacy and control of the assets. This technology eliminates the risks associated with centralized exchanges, making the exchange process more cost-effective and convenient.
